Gayatri Chanting

  • Om Bhur Bhuva Suvah
    Tat Savithur Varenyam
    Bhargo Devasya Dheemahi
    Dhiyo Yonah Prachodayat
  • We meditate on that most adorable, most desirable and most enchanting luster (effulgence) of our supreme Lord, who is our creator, inspirer and source of eternal Joy. May this light inspire and illumine our intellect (and dispel the darkness).

Gayatri Chanting

  • Sathya Sai speaks on benefits of Gayatri chanting
    • The effulgence of the Divine will descend on you and illumine your intellect and light your path when this mantra is chanted.
    • Gayatri is the bestower of all that is beneficial.
    • It is an essential requisite for the young because it ensures a bright and auspicious future for them.
    • The attitude of surrender will grow in us as we recite regularly the Gayatri manthra.
    • The Gayatri Mantra is not related to any particular sect, caste, idol, or institution. It is the torch of spiritual wisdom.
